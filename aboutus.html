Our Story

Who We Are

Koshmochon isn't just a clothing brand - we're a movement dedicated to helping men express their best selves through fashion.

Founded in Chennai in 2015, Koshmochon began as a small boutique with a big vision: to make premium men's fashion accessible without compromising on quality. Today, we've grown into one of South India's most trusted menswear brands, serving thousands of satisfied customers across the country.

Our team of fashion experts carefully curates each collection, blending timeless classics with contemporary trends to create versatile wardrobes for the modern man.
